## 2024-06-12 - Form Accessibility & Focus Management in Modals
**Learning:** Found instances where form `<label>` elements were visually indicating inputs but lacked programmatic association (`htmlFor` referencing the input's `id`), limiting screen reader support. Additionally, custom styled modal buttons (like the `SettingsModal` close and cancel buttons) lacked visible keyboard focus states (`focus-visible`).
**Action:** When adding or updating forms within modals or dialogues, explicitly associate `<label>` and `<input>` with `htmlFor` and `id`, ensure icon-only close buttons have an `aria-label`, and use Tailwind's `focus-visible` utilities (e.g., `focus-visible:ring-2`) on all interactive elements.
